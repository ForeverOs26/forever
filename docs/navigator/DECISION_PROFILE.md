\# Forever Decision Profile



Version: RC1



\## Purpose



The Forever Decision Profile is the permanent summary of what matters to the client.



It is created after the Navigator is completed.



It becomes the foundation for every recommendation.



\---



\## Profile Structure



\### Motivation



Why the client wants property.



\### Lifestyle



How the client wants to live.



\### Success Definition



What success means.



\### Budget



Comfortable investment range.



\### Timeline



Purchase readiness.



\### Risk Profile



Low



Medium



High



\### Investment Orientation



Primary residence



Holiday home



Investment



Mixed



\### Family Context



Single



Couple



Family



Retirement



\### Priority Ranking



The three strongest priorities identified by the Navigator.



\### Biggest Concern



Main uncertainty preventing the decision.



\---



\## Generated Assets



The profile generates:



\- Forever Story

\- Recommendation Context

\- Advisor Brief

\- Future conversations

\- Project matching



\---



\## Principles



Profiles never expire.



Profiles evolve over time.



Recommendations improve as the profile improves.



No recommendation should contradict the profile.
