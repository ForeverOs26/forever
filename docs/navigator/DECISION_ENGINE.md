\# Forever Navigator Decision Engine



Version: RC1



\## Goal



Transform client answers into a structured Forever Decision Profile.



The engine does not recommend projects directly.



Instead it produces a profile describing:



\- motivation

\- priorities

\- budget

\- timeline

\- concerns

\- lifestyle

\- investment profile



\---



\## Inputs



Navigator answers.



\---



\## Outputs



Forever Decision Profile



Forever Story



Recommendation Context



Advisor Context



\---



\## Core principles



Never recommend because of commission.



Reduce uncertainty.



Explain every recommendation.



Show trade-offs.



Respect uncertainty.



Avoid pressure.



\---



\## Recommendation priority



1\. Motivation

2\. Lifestyle fit

3\. Budget

4\. Timeline

5\. Investment potential

6\. Risk tolerance

7\. Long-term suitability



\---



\## Result



Every client receives:



\- Decision Profile

\- Forever Story

\- Project Recommendations

\- Advisor Notes
