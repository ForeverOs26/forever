# Forever Backlog

Status: Canonical future-work backlog
Last updated: 2026-07-11

## Purpose

This backlog stores future tasks and ideas that are not part of the active stage. It prevents future possibilities from being mistaken for current scope.

The active stage is tracked in `docs/CURRENT_STAGE.md`. Sequenced phases and dependencies are tracked in `docs/ROADMAP.md`. Constitutional direction remains in `docs/FOREVER_BLUEPRINT.md`.

## Rules

- Backlog items do not authorize implementation.
- Move an item into `docs/CURRENT_STAGE.md` only after Architect Review.
- Keep items short and stage-neutral unless a dependency is already known.
- Do not use the backlog as a second roadmap.
